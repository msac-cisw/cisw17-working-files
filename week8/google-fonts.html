Google Fonts

Google Fonts is a free service that provides access to a wide range of fonts for web use. It allows developers to easily integrate custom fonts into their websites, enhancing the visual appeal and user experience.

To use Google Fonts, you can visit the Google Fonts website, where you can browse and select fonts. Once you've chosen a font, you can embed it in your HTML by adding a link to the font in the head section of your document.

For example, to use the "Roboto" font, you would add the following line to your HTML:

<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Roboto:wght@400;700&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">

After adding the link, you can apply the font to your elements using CSS. For instance:

body {
  font-family: 'Roboto', sans-serif;
}

Google Fonts supports a wide variety of font styles and weights, making it easy to find the perfect font for your project. Additionally, it provides options for customizing the font loading behavior to optimize performance.

For more information and to explore the available fonts, visit the Google Fonts website.

Google Fonts is a valuable resource for web developers and designers, offering a simple and effective way to enhance the typography of their websites.
